1 Introduction
We begin by introducing the notions of Inﬂuence, Noise Stability & k-Juntas
of Boolean Functions. We then develop a recurrence relation for how to count
k-Juntas, and attempt to reason about their asymptotic growth. Following this,
we talk about the geometry of the Hamming Cube and its relation to Inﬂuence,
particularly the Majority function and monotone functions. We then conclude
with a section where we state and sketch the proof for a weak version of Arrow’s
Theorem, and end by stating the more robust (approximate) version using the
FKN Theorem.
